## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### 1. Test Title Variations
Before publishing, use the predictor to test 3-5 title variations. Pick the highest-scoring one.

### 2. Schedule for Peak Times
Use YouTube's scheduling feature to publish during optimal times, even if you're not available.

### 3. First 48 Hours Matter Most
YouTube's algorithm heavily weighs initial performance. Optimize everything before publishing.

### 4. Consistency > Perfection
Regular uploads with good optimization > irregular uploads with perfect optimization.

### 5. Match Audience Timezone
If your audience is global, pick the best compromise time or stagger uploads for different regions.

### 6. Weekend = Content Discovery
People browse more on weekends. Great time for new viewer acquisition.

### 7. Don't Sacrifice Quality
Optimization helps discovery, but content quality drives retention and long-term success.
